Creativity: What is it?

By observing and inquiring how users identity creative content online, the first phase of my research program studies how individuals acquires new information across computer-mediated platforms. This stage of the research informs how curated experiences in individualsâ€™ digital life shape their concept toward creativity. Up to date, research in creativity relies on definitions elaborated by scholars and domain experts. However, to design user-centered applications that augment creativity, there is the need to understand what the greater population consider as creativity. To attempt this objective, I conduct a series of focus groups and a longitudinal study , tracking and analyzing users' visit and view data on a mock content website.

User-Defined Creativity

What does it mean to be creative? Through a series of 14 focus groups, I asked participants to find examples of creative content and discuss why they were creative (or not).
